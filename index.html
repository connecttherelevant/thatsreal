That's Real! develops intellectual properties and produces music-centric shows. Our flagship, The Music Podcast, is a long-format YouTube series featuring in-depth conversations with music industry professionals. We aim to present an unbiased and truthful view of the music ecosystem.

"The Music Podcast,"

hosted by Tarsame Mittal, offers an immersive experience into the vibrant Indian music community. Tarsame's 20 years of industry expertise, the podcast aims to bridge the gap between fans and their favorite artists.

Each episode features key personalities from various music segments, including singers, lyricists, composers, engineers, DJs, and instrumentalists across genres and languages. Through candid conversations, viewers gain intimate insights into the lives and creative processes of these musicians. The podcast serves as a tribute to the profound impact of music and its creators on enthusiasts and society.
